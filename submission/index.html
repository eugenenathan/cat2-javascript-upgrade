My Story

More Than a Trainer —
A Partner in Progress

I'm Kofi Mensah, a certified personal trainer and sports nutritionist with over a decade of experience helping people across Nairobi — and online worldwide — achieve sustainable fitness transformations.

My own journey began after a career-ending injury as a competitive sprinter. Rebuilding my body taught me that true fitness is about consistency, education, and mindset — not quick fixes. I bring that lived experience to every client I work with.

Whether you're stepping into a gym for the first time or training for a marathon, I build programmes around your life, not the other way around.
